The problem

AI compliance is manual, expensive, and not built for AI.

Vanta and Drata handle IT compliance. But ISO 42001 requires 36 AI-specific controls they don't cover — bias assessment, model provenance, AI-specific threat mapping, explainability. The gap is a business risk.

Generic tools miss AI risks

Existing GRC platforms cross-map IT controls to AI standards. They can't distinguish a text-generation model from an image classifier, or map prompt injection to the correct ISO 42001 clause.

Evidence won't survive quantum

Every compliance report signed with RSA or ECDSA today becomes unverifiable when quantum computers arrive. Your audit trail has an expiry date.

Model access is a dealbreaker

Traditional compliance tools need admin access to infrastructure. CISOs spend months reviewing security before approving them. That's a barrier you can't afford.
